Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Vistro mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect certain information about your device, your interaction with the App, and information necessary to process your requests.

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email address and basic profile information.
  • Usage Data: We may log app usage data to help us improve the App's functionality.
  • Device Information: We may collect device identifiers and technical data for troubleshooting and analytics.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers (such as the Apple IDFA) to serve personalized ads. You can manage your ad tracking preferences in your iOS Settings.
  • Supabase: We use Supabase for database and authentication services to securely store your data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Understand and analyze how you use the App.
  • Show personalized advertisements (if you have opted-in to App Tracking Transparency).
  • Communicate with you regarding app updates or support.

4. Your Choices

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking on iOS). You can also request to delete your account and associated data at any time from within the App settings.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact our support team.
